Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data we collect

Account information (name, email), merchant configuration (UPI ID, webhook URLs), and encrypted Gmail/IMAP credentials for payment verification.

How we use IMAP credentials

Gmail App Passwords are encrypted at rest and used solely to read bank payment alert emails for matching UTR and amount to pending orders. We do not read personal emails unrelated to payment alerts.

Payment data

We store order IDs, amounts, UTR numbers, and transaction status. Customer payments go directly to your bank — we do not hold funds.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Webhook payloads are sent only to URLs you configure.
