Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you get started

What is UPIPays?

UPIPays is a Dynamic QR generating and payment verification service. You add your UPI ID and Gmail; we generate QR codes for your orders and verify payments automatically via bank email alerts.

Is UPIPays really 0% transaction fee?

Yes. We charge a flat monthly subscription for QR generation capacity — not a percentage of your sales. Your customers pay you directly via UPI to your bank account.

Do I need a payment gateway merchant account?

No. You only need a UPI ID linked to your bank account and a Gmail that receives bank payment alerts (HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Axis supported).

Which UPI apps do customers use?

All NPCI UPI apps — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay, and any bank UPI app.

How do I integrate with my website?

Use our REST API with HMAC authentication, or install a plugin for WooCommerce, aMember, and more. API keys are available in your dashboard after signup.

Is my Gmail password safe?

We recommend Gmail App Passwords (not your main password). Credentials are encrypted at rest on our servers and used only for reading bank alert emails.

Is UPIPays a licensed payment aggregator?

No. UPIPays provides Dynamic QR and verification technology. Payments go directly from customer to your bank via UPI — we do not hold your money.
